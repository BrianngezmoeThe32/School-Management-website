About Mafu Secondary School

"Empowering Minds, Building Character, Shaping Futures"

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal development and responsible citizenship in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and the holistic development of every learner.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical behavior
  • Respect: Valuing diversity and treating all with dignity
  • Responsibility: Being accountable for our actions and commitments
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and continuous improvement
  • Community: Building strong partnerships with parents and the community

Our History & Achievements

1995

Mafu Secondary School was established with just 150 students and 8 teachers. Our first principal was Mr. John Mafu, who envisioned a school that would serve the community with excellence.

2002

The school achieved a 100% pass rate for the first time in Grade 12 examinations, establishing our reputation for academic excellence.

2008

Expanded facilities with a new science laboratory and computer center, enabling us to offer enhanced STEM education.

2015

Celebrated 20 years of excellence with the opening of our multipurpose hall, funded through community donations and alumni support.

2020

Implemented our digital transformation initiative, providing tablets to all Grade 12 students and launching our online learning platform.

2023

Achieved our highest ever university placement rate of 92% for Grade 12 graduates, with students accepted into prestigious institutions nationwide.

Notable Achievements

Academic Excellence

Consistently achieving above national average pass rates in Grade 12 examinations for the past 15 years.

University Placement

Over 85% of our graduates secure placement in universities, colleges, or vocational training programs annually.

Community Engagement

Active involvement in community service projects, with students contributing over 5,000 volunteer hours annually.

Sports Excellence

Multiple provincial champions in athletics, soccer, and netball over the past decade.
